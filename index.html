About Us

Founded in 2013 by Barkathulla Khan, Le-Bon International LLC has grown to become a leading supplier of mobile phone spare parts. Located in the vibrant heart of Deira, we are proud to embody the meaning of our name, "the best."

At Le-Bon, we meticulously import top-tier spare parts from China, ensuring they meet our customer requirements. Our eye for detail and commitment to quality go hand-in-hand with our adherence to local laws.

We serve both business-to-business (B2B) and business-to-consumer (B2C) markets, predominantly catering to the Middle Eastern and African regions. As a one-stop shop for mobile phone spare parts, Le-Bon maintains competitive pricing while staying abreast of market trends.

Our robust volume capacity ensures high delivery efficiency, supporting our wide range of parts from top brands like Apple, Samsung, Oppo, Huawei, Redmi, Oneplus, Vivo, Moto, and Nokia.

Customer satisfaction is of paramount importance to us. To ensure a superior experience, we prioritize quick response times and maintain a staff with strong technical skills, friendly dispositions, and proficiency in multiple languages. Additionally, we assist with cargo services for customers requiring shipments.
